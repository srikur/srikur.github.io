# Emulator Development Guide

My goal is to have easy-to-follow guides for several of the most popular video game consoles from the 1980s, 1990s, and early 2000s. I will provide step-by-step instructions, along with source code in several languages: C++, Java, Python, Rust, OCaml, and Swift.

## Roadmap
1. Starting Out: CHIP-8
2. Gameboy / Gameboy Color
3. NES
4. SNES
5. Gameboy Advanced
6. Nintendo 64
7. PlayStation 1
