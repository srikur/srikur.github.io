Introduction Hopefully you’ve read the intro this series here and you know have at least a basic idea of what an emulator is about and what the roadmap is. To condense the plan into one sentence, our goal is to emulate the functionality of a game system programmatically by having an endless loop where we fetch low level instructions from ROMs (i.e. game cartridge binary dumps), execute those instructions, and repeat, updating the screen graphics as necessary.
